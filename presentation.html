02 / 07
Setting Strategic Direction

How I Set Goals
Using Data

  • Every quarter, I look at past results to understand what worked and what didn't
  • I also check trends and seasonality to see how performance changes over time
  • Based on this, I set clear goals for each platform
  • During the quarter, I track results weekly and adjust if needed

I use data to plan, track, and improve our social media strategy over time.
03 / 07
Creating Value

Creating Content That
Supports the Business

  • When the company wants to promote a location or campaign, I create content around it
  • For example, this post promotes the Dolomites as a travel destination
  • The goal is to inspire people and make them interested in booking
  • All of this is done organically, without using paid ads
I create content that helps the company reach its goals, not just get likes.
04 / 07
Business Development

Tracking What Actually
Brings Revenue

  • I use UTM links to track every post we publish
  • This helps me see how people move from social media to the website
  • I can also check if they complete a booking
  • This allows us to understand which content is really working
I don't just create content — I measure how it impacts the business.
05 / 07
Digital Project Scope

Planning Content in
a Structured Way

  • At the start of each week, I create a content calendar
  • Before that, I check trends and what is performing well
  • I plan content based on both data and company priorities
  • This helps keep everything organised and consistent
Good planning makes content better and more effective.
06 / 07
Conclusion

Strategy. Content. Impact.

Every part of this work connects: goals inform content, content drives traffic, and tracking proves the value. That loop is what makes social media a real business tool — not just a creative outlet.

Strategy

Quarterly goals set from data, tracked in real time, and sharpened each cycle.

Content

Organic posts aligned with campaigns, destinations, and business priorities — no ads needed.

Impact

UTM links and analytics connect every post back to website visits and bookings.

Social media isn't just about reach — when done right, it directly supports business growth.
